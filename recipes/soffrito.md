# Soffrito

- 1 cup onions, chopped finely
- 1 cup carrots, chopped finely
- 1 cup celery, chopped finely
- Salt
- Olive oil

Onions, carrots, and celery should be chopped equally fine. Sauté the vegetables in an excessive amount of olive oil and add salt. Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally until you can't see any distinction between the three anymore.

- 2 tbsp tomato paste

Add tomato paste and cook for 15 more minutes.
