# Meatballs

- 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp parsley
- Salt & pepper
- Parmezan (optional)

Combine milk & breadcrumbs and let sit for 10 minutes. Mix in the rest of the ingredients into a "panade".

- 500g ground pork/beef

Add the panade to the ground meat. More panade means fluffier meatballs, too much panade means flat meatballs. Less mixing also yields fluffier meatballs.

Either sauté the meatballs in a pan for a crunchier outside, or bake them in the oven (easier for large quantities).
